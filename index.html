Research Interests

I'm interested in liberalizations and extensions of traditional proof-theoretic semantics. In my PhD, I've developed various single-assumption single-conclusion systems where logical terms can be introduced in the scope of other connectives. I've investigated meaning-dependence between logical terms and discussed harmony for these systems. Now, I'm focusing on further extensions: bilateral, modal, and (more recently) probabilistic.
